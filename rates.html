Deals

  • Early bird bookings enjoy a 15% discount when booking at least 150 days in advance. Not applicable to Christmas and New Years
  • Last Minute bookings enjoy 10% discount when booked less than 10 days in advance of check-in date.
  • For new bookings only

Inclusions and conditions

You will enjoy the villa in complete privacy. We rent to only one guest or family at a time. If you use 1 or 2 bedrooms, the other bedrooms will not be rented to any other guests.

Inclusions

  • A la carte breakfast included, 2 maximum per bedroom occupied
  • Rates are for single or double occupancy per bedroom
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi for Whatsapp or emails provided, not for streaming
  • Check-in at 2:00 pm
  • Check-out at 11:00 am
  • Early check-in and late check-out ma be available upon request depending on occupancy

Terms and conditions

  • 50% deposit required upon booking confirmation.
  • Early Bird and Last Minute bookings to be paid in full and are non-refundable.
  • For any cancellations at least 61 days from date of arrival, the deposit will be refunded in full less US$100 in administration fees.
  • For any cancellations within 60 days, the deposit will be forfeited.
