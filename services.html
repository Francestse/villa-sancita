Your Own Private Cooks

Our dining room seats 6 comfortably where your included breakfast is served each morning by our gentle staff. If you want another meal in, our talented cooks are happy to prepare homemade Balinese dishes that are hard to find outside of traditional Balinese compounds. We charge only for the actual costs of groceries plus a nominal service fee and government tax. Depending on your menu, the costs of an average meal would be about USD 7-10 per person.

Child Minding

We can arrange babysitting and baby cots at extra charges. Please let us know your needs and we’ll be happy to organize.

Laundry Services

Laundry services are quick and inexpensive in Bali. Our staff are happy to bring your laundry to be cleaned daily.

Spa Massages

We have a custom massage bed in the villa for your personal use. Our team can arrange for relaxing spa therapies that are convenient to your schedule.

Tours of Bali

Should you wish to explore the island’s mystical volcanoes, picturesque rice fields and fabled temples, Villa Sancita’s friendly manager will be happy to arrange transport or tours. We would highly recommend taking an authentic cooking class at www.tresnbali.com

Transportation

We are located only 3-5 minutes drive from Central Ubud. We can arrange car transfers, and bicycle and motorbike rentals during your stay at additional charges.
