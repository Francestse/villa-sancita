Location and Services

Villa Sancita is located at the end of a small laneway in the quiet villa of Junjunjan, only 5 minutes drive North of main street Ubud. The villa faces an uninterrupted view of swaying coconut tress and verdant rice fields. Life is unhurried in this peaceful rice-farming neighbourhood of Balinese families and traditional temples. Yet within a short walk are signs of more modern times – a growing number of excellent warungs (local cafés) or dip down into the rice fields for a walk from ancient days.
